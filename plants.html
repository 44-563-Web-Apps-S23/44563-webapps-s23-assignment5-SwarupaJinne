As I resize the browser, the row containing the plant names breaks first. This is because the col-md class is used for the second row, which means that the divs take up more space than the col-sm-2 divs in the first row. As the screen size reduces, the second row divs become too large to fit on a single row, and the browser stacks them vertically. Meanwhile, the first row divs still fit within the available space and remain on a single row.
